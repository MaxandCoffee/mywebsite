ABOUT ME

Everyday is an Adventure

A traveler by nature, I came to web development through an untraditional path. I earned my BA and MA in English and became a professor. Dissatisfied by the learning management systems used by my institutions, I created course websites that were more engaging and intuitive to use for my students. I then moved into professional advising in the STEM department where I immersed myself in the various coding languages. Before I knew it, I found myself more excited to come home and code than I was going to work. In the evenings and late nights, with a hot cuppa and my laptop blazing bright, Max & Coffee was born.

Max Hallsett


Welcome to my website. I believe that applications should be a pleasure to use across all platforms and devices. I create welcoming and intuitive user experiences that are responsive and accessible. Whether I'm hand-coding a custom website in HTML/5, CSS/3, and Javascript, or developing a fully functional cross platform application with Angular2 and Typescript, I take a content first approach and never lose site of the user.
